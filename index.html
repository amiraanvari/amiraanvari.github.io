I am a PhD candidate in linguistics at Institut Jean-Nicod, in Paris. My supervisors are Philippe Schlenker and Benjamin Spector. I am expected to graduate by December 2019. My research involves formal analysis of natural language semantics (which meanings are associated with which sentences, and how) and pragmatics (which inferences are associated with which utterances, and how). A detailed outline of my current research can be found in my dissertation's abstract.

My partner, Leila Mirzagholi, is a PhD candidate at the Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics in Munich. To her, I look like this.
